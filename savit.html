The Design Brief

Design a Web based budget planner that allows users to manage their finances. Users should be able to create easily and adjust budgets to reach their savings goals. The budget planner should give users a conversational summary of their spending habits across different periods (for example, on a weekly or monthly basis) and recommend changes to spending habits.

The Solution

After understanding the task and the demands of the design challenge. I sought out to create a functional UI experience that could be familiar with users and easy to navigate. The home screen was designed to have your current balance displayed on your dashboard whilst having access to create a new budget for your plans, easy access to your invoices like your subscriptions and many more.

Conclusion

The entire design process of this design challenge was an entirely new but rewarding experience for me as a designer. It gave me the opportunity to research the savings culture, to better understand their needs and the pivotal aspects of maintaining a saving habit/lifestyle.

I had to lean on resources available to make this project and I learned the newer ways to see problems and how to solve them. On my next iteration, I would like to work better on the flows for this design and incorporate it on the app.
